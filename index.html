About me

I'm Amer Hallaq from Palestine, a Software Engineer with a background in Mechatronics Engineering. After graduation, I have worked in Oman, Kazakhstan, and Palestine. My skills in both electrical and mechanical fields have enabled me to take on various roles in multiple industries, including oil and gas, solar energy, and BIM engineering. Since 2020, I have been working as a BIM Design Engineer, which inspired me to expand my knowledge and skills in software development. To pursue this interest, I joined bootcamps in Python and Node.js, complemented by self-learning through Coursera, Udemy, and other online resources. My goal is to integrate my BIM engineering experience with software development to design and develop new tools that enhance productivity. A year ago, I transitioned to a full-time Software Engineer role, where I have designed and implemented various projects, utilizing both front-end and back-end skills.
